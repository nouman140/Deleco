## 🎯 Mission & Vision

**Unlock Affordable Gourmet Delights while Reducing Food Waste**

We believe that great food shouldn't go to waste. Deleco partners with local restaurants, bakeries, and food suppliers to rescue high-quality surplus meals and deliver them directly to your doorstep at affordable prices.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🍲 **Surplus Meal Rescue** – Gourmet meals that would otherwise go to waste
- 🚚 **Doorstep Delivery** – Convenient delivery to your home or office
- 💰 **Affordable Pricing** – Gourmet food at reduced costs
- 🌍 **Eco-Conscious** – Reduce food waste and your carbon footprint
- 📱 **User-Friendly Platform** – Easy ordering process
